About MindMapr

MindMapr is a modern learning platform inspired by Pinterest’s discovery model, combined with the power of mind mapping and visual connections.

Our Mission

We believe learning becomes easier when ideas are visual, connected, and interactive. MindMapr helps students, educators, and lifelong learners transform concepts into visual maps that are easy to remember and fun to explore.

How It Works

Each “pin” on MindMapr represents a concept, infographic, mind map node, or educational video. You can connect pins into meaningful visual structures that represent how you understand a topic.

AI-Powered Concept Suggestions

Our AI engine learns your topic and recommends related concepts, helping you expand your mind maps faster. This makes research, revision, and deep learning easier than ever.
